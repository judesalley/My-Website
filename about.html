WHO WE ARE

OUR MISSION

Straight-A Tutoring offers affordable tutoring and test prep in all subjects to kids in disadvantaged communities. Our highly-educated tutors will give these students the individual attention they need to address their weaknesses and improve their performance in the classroom. Our clients will receive support that they might not get at school or at home, helping to increase their motivation and academic performance. Our aim is to reduce academic underperformance among people in underserved communities and close the gap in academic achievement between rich and poor.
